## 8. FAQ SECTION

**Q: Do you make eggless cakes?**
A: Yes! We offer eggless cake options. Please mention this when placing your order.

**Q: How far in advance should I order a custom cake?**
A: We recommend ordering at least 2–3 days in advance for custom designs. For larger or more intricate cakes, 4–5 days notice is ideal.

**Q: What is the minimum order size for a custom cake?**
A: We typically start from 500g / 1 kg. Contact us for specific requirements.

**Q: Do you deliver?**
A: Yes, we offer delivery from all 4 outlets. Contact your nearest branch for delivery radius and charges.

**Q: Can I do a last-minute order?**
A: We try our best! Call your nearest outlet and we'll let you know what's possible same-day.

**Q: Do you do wedding cakes?**
A: Absolutely — multi-tier wedding cakes are a specialty. We'd love to be part of your big day.
